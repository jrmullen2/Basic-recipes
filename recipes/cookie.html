Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Making desserts is always a hassle. Most of the time it is more convenient to buy desserts from the store. However, there is one dessert worth making. This recipe involves making delectable and delicious chocolate chip cookies. This page will display the ingredients required and then will provide you with the necessary steps to make this delightful dessert.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
