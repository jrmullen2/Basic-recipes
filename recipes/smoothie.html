Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Description

In need of a refreshing and rejuvenating beverage? Well then the Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie might be just what you need! In this recipe we are going to cover the ingredients and of course the steps it takes to make this wonderful drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Prepare your banana and strawberries to put into the blender.
  2. Add all the ingredients into the blender and start the blender.
  3. Blend the mixture until it has the consistency you want.
